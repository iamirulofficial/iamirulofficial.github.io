SuperPDF Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

SuperPDF is designed as an on-device PDF scanner and document tool. We do not sell personal information, track users across apps or websites, or include analytics SDKs in the app.

Information We Collect

SuperPDF does not collect personal information on our servers. Documents, scans, photos selected for conversion, generated PDFs, extracted text, OCR output, typed text, pasted text, and dictated speech text are processed on your device and saved locally unless you choose to share or export them.

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you choose to scan documents.
  • Microphone: Used only when you choose Speech to PDF dictation.
  • Speech Recognition: Used only to convert your dictated speech into text for PDF creation.
  • Photo Library: Used only when you choose photos to convert into a PDF or export images.
  • Files/Documents: Used only when you select PDFs to import, edit, merge, split, compress, secure, or convert.

How Documents Are Handled

PDF operations such as scan, create PDF from text, speech dictation, paste-to-PDF, merge, split, rotate, reorder, watermark, page numbering, compression, password protection, text extraction, and OCR are performed locally on the device. SuperPDF does not upload your documents to a server in this build.

Third Parties

SuperPDF integrates the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to show occasional full-screen interstitial ads to non-Pro users. AdMob may collect the device's advertising identifier, IP address, and basic interaction data for ad delivery and measurement, subject to the choice you make in iOS's App Tracking Transparency prompt. Declining tracking still serves ads, but only non-personalised ones. See Google's privacy policy for AdMob's specific data practices.

Apple StoreKit handles the optional one-time "SuperPDF Pro" purchase. Apple may receive purchase confirmation data; SuperPDF only sees a Pro entitlement flag.

Beyond AdMob and Apple StoreKit, SuperPDF does not use third-party analytics, attribution, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

SuperPDF is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
